About

I am in my last year as an undergraduate at the University of California, San Diego. In June 2018, I will receive a B.S. in Cognitive Science, Specialization in Design and Interaction.

My interdisciplinary major has allowed me to acquire an academic background in psychology, neuroscience, and design. I believe the best designs are the ones that take into account how the users' minds process and function, and create a user experience that is intuitive and satisfying.

Studyer

For COGS 120: Interaction Design, I worked on a team to create a mobile application in response to a need we discovered students had.


The application was a centralized way of accessing course information: announcements, deadlines, grades, and course resources, while staying informed about their status and progress.


We all contributed equally in the storyboarding and wireframing process. I and one teammate handled the development timeline and user testing.

I personally contributed the most to the simplicity of the homepage and clarity of the icons.
